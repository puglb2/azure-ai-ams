Q: What’s the difference between therapy and psychiatry?
A: Therapy focuses on talk-based strategies and skills. Psychiatry can prescribe and manage medications. Many people benefit from both.

Q: How do I know if I need both?
A: If symptoms impact daily life (work/school/sleep/appetite) or persist >2–4 weeks, consider therapy + psychiatry together.
